Research Highlights

ExpertsMedSAM — Mixture-of-Experts architecture for accelerating medical image segmentation. Dynamic feature routing to specialized experts enables efficient inference on edge devices.
MICCAI STS 2024 Challenge — First semi-supervised instance-level tooth segmentation benchmark across panoramic X-ray and CBCT. SSL improves over supervised baselines by 60+ points.
GOMPS — Global attention-based ophthalmic image measurement and postoperative appearance prediction system. Deployed for clinical decision support.
STS 2023 Challenge — Semi-supervised tooth segmentation benchmark with 6500+ panoramic images and 580+ CBCT volumes. Top teams achieve 93+ Dice on 2D tasks.
